A legacy of commercial trust

From affected stock to a clear, accountable outcome.

Shaw & Company combines inspection experience, operational coordination and a deep resale network to move stock responsibly across India.

Established in 1990 and based in College Street, Kolkata, the business supports insurance-related assignments alongside salvage, disposal, liquidation, wholesale and retail operations.

Our reach across multiple product categories allows us to assess each lot practically, coordinate movement efficiently and connect it with the most suitable recovery channel.

What we do

One experienced partner across the stock lifecycle.

From the first inspection to final movement and value recovery, our work is structured around clarity, speed and dependable coordination.

01

Insurance Stock Inspection

On-site stock verification, condition assessment, photographic documentation and coordination support for insurance-related assignments.

02

Pickup & Pan-India Logistics

Planned pickup, loading, movement and delivery of affected or surplus stock through a dependable nationwide operating network.

03

Salvage & Disposal Management

Practical recovery and disposal solutions for damaged, unclaimed and claim-related goods, handled with clear commercial coordination.

04

Liquidation & Surplus Stock

Bulk acquisition and channel-based movement of retail liquidation, corporate surplus, discontinued and slow-moving inventory.

05

Wholesale & Retail Distribution

06

Customs, Port & Transport Stock

Experienced handling of customs goods, port trust materials, transport surplus and other institutional stock categories.
